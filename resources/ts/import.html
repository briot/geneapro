Importing data into GeneaProve

At this stage, GeneaProve is mostly read-only. As such, we recommend importing data from an other genealogy software. In particular, the authors have tested imported from Gramps or Roots Magic.

Most genealogy software are capable of exporting your data as a GEDCOM file, which GeneaProve can then import to get you started.

GeneaProve could not import your file.

If your GEDCOM data contains information you wish to keep private, you can use the script gedcom_anon.py to anonymize the data before you send it to us.
